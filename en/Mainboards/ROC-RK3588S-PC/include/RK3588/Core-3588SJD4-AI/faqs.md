## About DIP Switch

DIP switch location:
![](../../../rk3588_img/ROC-RK3588S-PC/power_dip_switch.jpg)

* When DIP switch is `ON`, device will boot automatically after being connected to the power supply.
* When DIP switch is `OFF`, device will not boot until you press `Power` button.
* DIP switch is `ON` by default.
