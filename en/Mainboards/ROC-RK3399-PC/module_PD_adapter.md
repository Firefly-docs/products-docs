# [PD adapter](https://www.firefly.store/products/pd-charger-type-c-interface-quick-charge)

## Product Parameter

* **product**: Type-C PD power adapter
* **input standard**: AC 110-240V
* **output standard**: PD 2.0 15V/3A 5V/3A，9V/3A，12V/2A，14.5V/2A
* **output power**: 29W
* **Quick charge agreement**: PD2.0

**Note:** ROC-RK3399-PC all-in-one machine needs a power supply of 14.5v /2A for normal operation. If the current is lower than 2A, it may restart abnormally due to too small current. In order to ensure the normal operation of the development board, please use a power supply with a voltage of 14.5v and a current of 2A~3A.
