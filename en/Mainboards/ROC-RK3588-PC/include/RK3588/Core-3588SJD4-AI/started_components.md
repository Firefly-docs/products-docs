## Introduction
 
**ROC-RK3588-PC** uses Rockchip's flagship AIoT chip RK3588S, built on an advanced 8nm LP process. It features an octa-core 64-bit CPU with a clock speed of up to 2.4GHz, an intergrated ARM Mali-G610 MP4 quad-core GPU, and a built-in AI-accelerator NPU delivering 6 TOPS of computing power. RK3588S optimizes performance for diverse AI applications.

![](../../../rk3588_img/ROC-RK3588-PC/core_front.jpg)  
      

[AIO-3588SJD4-AI]() is made up with Core-3588SJD4-AI and Motherboard MB-JD4-BM1688. AIO-3588SJD4-AI is a complete high-performance industrial mainboard with rich expansion interfaces enabling direct intergration into various smart devices to accelerate product deployment. More interface details at [Interface Definition](interface_definition.md)。
  
![](../../../rk3588_img/ROC-RK3588-PC/aio_front.jpg)
