# [USB Power Adapter](https://www.firefly.store/products/5v2a-us-adapter-3c-fcc-ce)

### Product Parameter
* **product**	    ：USB Power Adapter（USA Standard）
* **input voltage** ：110-240VAC
* **output voltage**：5V DC
* **output current**：2A
* **rated power**   ：10W
* **DC interface**  ：USB

**Note:** ROC-RK3566-PC  needs a power supply of 5V/2A for normal operation. If the current is lower than 2A, it may restart abnormally due to too small current. In order to ensure the normal operation of the development board, please use a power supply with a voltage of 5v and Current greater than 2A
