# Product introduction

Based on Rockchip new generation of flagship AIOT chip -- RK3588, the EC-A3588SJD4-AI features an 8nm LP process and an 8-core (Cortex-A76x4+Cortex-A55x4) 64-bit CPU with up to 2.4 GHz.Integrated ARM Mali-G610MP4 quad-core GPU, built-in AI accelerator NPU,can provide 6Tops computing power, support the mains, support up to 32 GB of memory, support 8K video codec and multiple formats of video input and output, support multiple operating systems can be applied to ARM PC, edge computing,cloud server, intelligent NVR and other fields.
