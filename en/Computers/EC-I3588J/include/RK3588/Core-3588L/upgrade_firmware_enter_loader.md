* Disconnect the power adapter first:
* Type-C data cable connects one end to the host and the other end to the development board.
![](../../../rk3588_img/EC-I3588J/upgrade_otg_interface.jpg)

* Press the `RECOVERY` button on the device and hold.
* Different hardware versions may result in slightly different button positions. Please refer to the actual screen printing for accuracy.

![](../../../rk3588_img/EC-I3588J/upgrade_recovery_reset.png)

* Connect to the power supply.
* About two seconds later, release the `RECOVERY` button.
