# Power adapter

## Product parameter

* Product parameter：Power Adapter
* Model：HHT108A-120700
* Input：AC100-240V1.8A 50/60Hz
* Output：DC12V-7A

**Note：** AIO-3588SJD4  normal operation of the fusioncube requires 12V/7A power supply. If the current is lower than 7A, abnormal restart may occur. To ensure normal operation of the development board, please use a power supply with 12V and 7A current.
