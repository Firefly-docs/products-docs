# Introduction
[iCore-3562JQ](https://www.firefly.store/products/icore-3562jq-cost-effective-industrial-core-board) is powered by the Rockchip RK3562J, a high-performance, low-power quad-core 64-bit Cortex-A53 application processor designed specifically for consumer electronics. It features a frequency of up to 1.2 GHz and comes equipped with an integrated 3D GPU, offering compatibility with Open GL ES 1.1/2.0/3.2, Open CL 2.0, and Vulkan 1.1. This chip also provides a 13M ISP for advanced image signal processing and supports HDR functionality. The RK3562J is built for wide-temperature resilience, reliably operating from -40°C to 85°C, making it ideal for a variety of industrial applications.

![](../../../rk3562_img/iCore-3562JQ/iCore-3562JQ.jpg)

[AIO-3562JQ](https://www.firefly.store/products/aio-3562jq-cost-effective-industrial-mainboard-operating-temperature-40-85) is combined by iCore-3562JQ and MB-Q-RK3562. The BTB interface provides more powerful transmission. This core board's industrial-grade stability ensures reliable long-term operation within a temperature range of -40°C to 85°C. With an array of interfaces, it supports various video output options, multiple cameras, Gigabit Ethernet, WiFi, and 5G/4G expansion. Compatible with multiple operating systems, it is widely used in intelligent digital signage, industrial control systems, industrial PLCs, energy data concentrators, smart healthcare, self-service terminals, and more.
